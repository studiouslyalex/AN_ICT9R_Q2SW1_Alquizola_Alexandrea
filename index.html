My Favorite Artists

Ever since I was a kid, I had an inclination to art and music. Whenever music is set to the beat around every corner of my house or school, I love to groove and move to the beat. The styles and influences from these artists—ranging from classical, folk, jazz and pop—eventually inspired me to take up music lessons and compose music of my own.


Original Filipino Music

Ben&Ben

Ben&Ben, formely known as The Benjamins, is a Filipino indie folk-pop band emerging from Manila. They were formed in 2016 by Paolo and Miguel Benjamin Guico.

Bini

Bini is a Filipino pop group under Star Magic of ABS-CBN. The compose of members Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena.

Maki

Maki is a alternative R&B, pop, and OPM artist and singer-songwriter based in Quezon City. He gained traction with his pop hits "Saan?," "Kailan?" and "Dilaw."

Rivermaya

Rivermaya is a Filipin alternative rock band formed in 1994. It was popularly composed of members Nathan Azarcon, Rico Blanco, Mark Escueta, and Bamboo Mañalac.


Western Music

Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is an alternative rock band composed of Dan Reynolds (vocals), Wayne Sermon (guitar), and Ben McKee (bass). They have received a Grammy for their song "Radioactive" in 2014.

Billy Joel

Billy Joel is an American singer-songwriter and pianist dubbed as the "Piano Man," after his 1973 masterpiece of the same name. To add, he is a six-time Grammy winner.

Laufey

Laufey is an Icelandic singer-songwriter, record producer, and multi-instrumentalist. Additionally, she won the Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album in the 2024 Grammy Awards.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter and musician recognized for her artistic reinventions, diverse discorgraphy, and biographical songwriting. Also, she won 14 Grammy Awards.
